A Google Ads freelancer specializes in managing and optimizing Google Ads campaigns for businesses. This includes keyword research, ad copy creation, bid management, budget allocation, conversion tracking setup, and performance analysis. The goal is to maximize return on ad spend (ROAS) and achieve specific marketing objectives, such as lead generation or e-commerce sales, by leveraging Google's advertising platform.

The primary difference lies in scale, cost, and personal attention. A freelancer, like myself, offers a direct, personalized service, often at a lower cost than an agency due to fewer overheads. You work directly with the expert managing your campaigns, ensuring consistent communication and a deep understanding of your business goals. Agencies, on the other hand, have larger teams, which can offer a broader range of services but might involve multiple points of contact and potentially higher fees. For many businesses, a freelancer offers a more agile and cost-effective solution with dedicated expertise.

My pricing is tailored to the specific needs and scale of your Google Ads campaigns, ensuring you only pay for the services that provide genuine value to your business. Typically, I offer flexible pricing models, including a percentage of ad spend or a flat monthly fee, depending on the complexity and scope of the project. During this call, we can discuss your current Google Ads performance, your business objectives, and I can provide a transparent proposal that aligns with your budget and goals. My aim is always to deliver exceptional ROI, making my services an investment, not just an expense.

Vinterior Case Study

E-commerce furniture marketplace

Industry

E-commerce

Project Goal

Increase sales while maintaining ROAS

Duration

12 Months

About Vinterior

Vinterior is a leading online marketplace for vintage and antique furniture, art, and home decor. They connect buyers with independent sellers, offering a curated selection of unique pieces. Their growth relies heavily on effective online advertising to reach a broad audience of interior design enthusiasts and collectors.

My Role & Strategy

I was engaged to optimize Vinterior's Google Ads account with a primary focus on increasing sales volume while maintaining a healthy Return on Ad Spend (ROAS). My strategy involved a multi-faceted approach:

  • Re-structuring Shopping campaigns for better product segmentation.
  • Implementing advanced Performance Max strategies for broader reach.
  • Refining keyword targeting and negative keywords for Search campaigns.
  • Optimizing bid strategies to align with ROAS targets.
  • Enhancing ad copy and creative assets to improve click-through rates.

Key Challenges

  • Highly competitive market for furniture.
  • Managing a diverse product catalog.
  • Ensuring positive ROAS amidst scaling.

Results Achieved

  • +283% increase in avg. monthly sales via Google Ads.
  • Maintained a strong ROAS, ensuring profitable growth.
  • Improved visibility for high-value product categories.

Summary

By implementing strategic campaign optimizations and focusing on data-driven decisions, I successfully helped Vinterior significantly increase their monthly sales from Google Ads, proving the effectiveness of targeted and well-managed advertising in a competitive e-commerce landscape. This partnership highlighted the importance of continuous optimization and a deep understanding of the client's business objectives.

Mortgage Broker UK Case Study

Financial Services - Lead Generation

Industry

Financial Services

Project Goal

Increase lead volume, lower CPA

Duration

24 Months

About Mortgage Broker UK

Mortgage Broker UK is a prominent independent mortgage advisory firm helping individuals and families secure the best mortgage deals. Their business thrives on a consistent flow of high-quality leads, which they primarily generate through online channels.

My Role & Strategy

My engagement focused on transforming their Google Ads campaigns from generating moderate leads at a higher cost to delivering a high volume of quality leads at a significantly reduced CPA. Key strategies included:

  • Extensive keyword expansion and negative keyword refinement to target high-intent users.
  • Developing compelling ad copy that resonated with potential clients.
  • Implementing robust conversion tracking to accurately measure lead quality and source.
  • Continuous A/B testing of landing pages and ad variations.
  • Optimizing bidding strategies for maximum lead generation efficiency.

Key Challenges

  • Highly regulated and competitive financial sector.
  • Generating high-quality leads, not just volume.
  • Adapting to evolving market conditions and Google Ads policies.

Results Achieved

  • Increased leads from ~30 to 350-400 per month.
  • Reduced Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) from ~£25 to ~£20.
  • Consistent pipeline of qualified mortgage enquiries.

Summary

Through strategic and consistent optimization, I successfully scaled Mortgage Broker UK's lead generation efforts by over 10x while simultaneously lowering their CPA. This long-term partnership demonstrates the power of a dedicated, data-driven approach to Google Ads in the financial services sector, delivering sustained growth and a strong return on investment.

SaaS Startup Case Study

Software as a Service - Full Funnel Optimization

Industry

SaaS

Project Goal

Optimize full customer journey for sign-ups

Duration

18 Months

About SaaS Startup

This innovative SaaS startup provides a cutting-edge platform designed to streamline operations for small to medium-sized businesses. Their growth model relies on attracting new users to sign up for free trials and ultimately convert into paid subscriptions, making efficient and effective digital advertising crucial.

My Role & Strategy

My role extended beyond standard Google Ads management to encompass a holistic optimization of the customer journey. This involved:

  • Deep analysis and optimization of Google Search and Display campaigns.
  • Collaborating on landing page optimization to improve conversion rates for trial sign-ups.
  • Implementing advanced server-side conversion tracking for more accurate data.
  • Setting up offline conversion tracking to attribute sales from CRM data back to ads.
  • Integrating CRM analytics to understand the full customer lifecycle and identify valuable user segments.
  • Providing insights for product messaging and user experience improvements.

Key Challenges

  • Complex conversion funnel from trial to paid.
  • Attributing value across multiple touchpoints.
  • Ensuring data accuracy for informed decisions.

Results Achieved

  • Significantly improved trial sign-up conversion rates.
  • Enhanced visibility into full customer journey and ROI.
  • Data-driven insights leading to product and marketing improvements.

Summary

This project was a testament to the power of integrating Google Ads with broader marketing and sales analytics. By working closely with the SaaS startup on everything from ad campaigns to landing pages and CRM integration, I was able to provide a comprehensive optimization strategy that went "above and beyond", resulting in more efficient customer acquisition and a clearer understanding of their marketing performance.
