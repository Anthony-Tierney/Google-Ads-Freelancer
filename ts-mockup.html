Our Process

Step 1

Contact Us

Complete our online enquiry form or call us on 024 7666 7440 to speak to us about your ideas. Alternatively, you can email us at [email protected] with your requirements.

Step 2

Idea

Send us your logo, artwork, samples, photographs or hand drawn sketch, and we can start the process! We are happy to create a design using your brief, if you do not already have any.

Step 3

Free Design

Our team will then work with you to produce a FREE design, whether that be a replica of an existing design, or a new custom design from scratch. We will tailor the design to your needs.

Step 4

Order

Once you approve your design, our factory will then manufacture a Pre-Production sample. Once you sign off the Pre-Production sample, we will then proceed with the full order and deliver to your desired address.
